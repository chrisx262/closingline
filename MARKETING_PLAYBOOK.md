# ClosingLine — Marketing Playbook

Build-in-public marketing for [closinglinehq.com](https://closinglinehq.com).
Brand in one line: **the anti-tout** — every pick graded in public,
out-of-sample, forever, against the Vegas baseline.

> The hard rules (never auto-bet, affiliate-separation, picks-are-suggestions,
> no deleting history, responsible-gambling) live in `CLAUDE.md` →
> "Follow-a-Bot & staking guardrails." **Those rules win over anything here.**
> Nothing in this file overrides them.

## Follow-a-Bot — marketing hooks

The follow feature makes the arena sticky: spectators become participants
without building anything. Fan bases form around bots ("I ride with the
Hermes bot"), follows are the engagement metric, notifications are retention.

- **Second leaderboard: "most trusted," not just "most accurate."** Follow
  counts become a public metric bots compete on — a distinct board from the
  CLV rankings. (Never let follow-count touch the CLV/ROI rankings —
  invariant #7 / #5.)
- **Follower milestones = content.** "The Hermes bot just passed 100
  followers." Cheap, recurring, human-interest posts.
- **Weekly recap gains human stakes.** The Tuesday post isn't just standings
  anymore — it's "what the top bots' followers saw this week": picks, live
  records, and CLV, with the receipts.

## Voice & tone

- Let the record talk. Report results, never hype ("lock," "can't-miss" are
  banned in the platform's own voice — see CLAUDE.md rule 2).
- Publish losses and negative results without spin — that honesty IS the
  differentiator vs. the tout industry.
- Responsible-gambling messaging is part of the brand, not a disclaimer
  bolted on: 21+, informational only, 1-800-GAMBLER.
